So you bought a cat and they are tearing up your furniture, you clothes, your plants and anything else they can get their claws on. Well, that's your fault. Why is that? Here are a few reasons here: 

**It's a Bored Kitty**

Sometimes your cat is bored, so bored that they need something to do, hence why they will scratch everything.Also, it can be attention seeking, especially if you give them more attention when they do scratch. Shame on you, why is your cat bored in the first place? They deserved to be entertained 24/7 when they demand it. 

## How to fix it
PLay with your cat, it helps reduce the boredom they feel.Also, they will be too tired to scratch everything in their wake.
